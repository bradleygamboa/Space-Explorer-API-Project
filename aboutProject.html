About Page

For this project, our group sought to take advantage of NASA's effort to make the results of its research more accessible to the general public. As NASA generates more than 15 TB of data each day, distribution of this data represents a formidable task for which NASA is relying in part on APIs. The goal of this project is to help NASA amke its data more available and increase interest in exploration of our solar system. To this end, our webpage lets users learn about our galaxy through the NASA image of the day, the unmanned Mars exploration missions through Mars rover images, and asteroids and meteoroids via NASA's tracking of Near-Earth-Objects (NEOs).

Our group is comprised of Kevin Angeles, Bradley Gamboa, Tobias Uriegas & Drew Wagner. Though each member contributed to a wide variety of projects the HTML framework, styling and overall aesthetic feel of the site was attained largely through the efforts of Bradley and Tobias while Kevin and Drew primarily worked on the Javascript used for NASA API data manipulation.

Happy Exploring.
